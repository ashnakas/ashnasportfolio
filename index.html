About Me

My Approach to Design

I believe great design is invisible‚Äîit removes barriers rather than creating them. My work centers on accessibility not as an afterthought, but as a fundamental design principle that makes products better for everyone.

Through research, prototyping, and iterative testing, I create interfaces that are both beautiful and functional, ensuring that digital experiences work for users of all abilities.

Core Skills

  • Design: Figma, Sketch, Adobe Creative Suite, Prototyping, Wireframing, User Research, Usability Testing
  • Development: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React, TypeScript, Node.js, Git, Responsive Design, Web Standards
  • Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AAA, ARIA, Screen Reader Testing, Keyboard Navigation, Color Contrast, Inclusive Design
  • Research: User Interviews, Surveys, A/B Testing, Analytics, Journey Mapping, Persona Development

Design Philosophy

  • Accessibility First: Designing for edge cases creates better solutions for everyone. I prioritize accessibility from the start, not as a compliance checkbox.
  • User-Centered Process: Real users, real feedback, real impact. I ground every design decision in research and testing with diverse user groups.
  • Thoughtful Innovation: Good design balances creativity with functionality. I explore new interaction patterns while maintaining usability and inclusivity.
