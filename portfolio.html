Design Philosophy

I approach every project with three core principles:

  • Accessibility First: Digital experiences should be usable by everyone. I design with WCAG standards, keyboard navigation, screen readers, and diverse user needs at the forefront.
  • User-Centered Design: Technology serves people, not the other way around. Through user research, testing, and iterative design, I ensure solutions actually solve real problems.
  • Thoughtful Innovation: Good design balances creativity with functionality. I explore new interaction patterns and technologies while maintaining usability and inclusivity.
